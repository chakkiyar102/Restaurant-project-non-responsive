Bringing Joy to people and planet NYC.



PLANET Burger, a plant-based fast-casual restaurant, is on a mission to bring joy not only to people but also to our planet, starting from its locations in New York City (NYC). Founded with a vision to offer delicious, sustainable, and healthier alternatives to traditional fast food, PLANET Burger focuses on crafting mouth-watering burgers, sandwiches, and sides that are entirely plant-based. This initiative reflects their commitment to environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and the health of their customers.

Our NYC locations serve as vibrant hubs for both locals and visitors looking to enjoy flavorful meals that are kind to the earth. By choosing plant-based ingredients, PLANET Burger aims to reduce carbon footprint, conserve water resources, and promote biodiversity, aligning with their goal of making a positive impact on the planet. As they expand, PLANET Burger continues to innovate in the culinary space, proving that fast food can be both delicious and responsible.
